Naxos

Xerjoff

Xerjoff Naxos is a fragrance that embodies the richness of Italian heritage, blending classic elegance with modern sensuality. Inspired by the grandeur of Sicily, Naxos opens with a burst of fresh citrus-bergamot, lemon, and lavender-creating a bright and invigorating introduction. As the scent develops, warm and spicy heart notes of cinnamon, honey, and tobacco take center stage, evoking a sense of luxury and depth. This sophisticated blend is both comforting and bold, reminiscent of the golden warmth of Mediterranean evenings.
